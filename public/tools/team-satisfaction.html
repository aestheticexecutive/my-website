Define what great looks like

Before assessing where you are, get clear on where you want to go. These prompts help you define your target — the team culture and satisfaction level you're building toward.

Ask your team

Run a short anonymous survey or roundtable with your team using these questions. Record the key themes and responses below — then compare to your own self-assessment.

Suggested survey or roundtable questions

·What motivates you most at work right now?
·When do you feel most connected to the team?
·What's one thing I could do more of — or less of — to improve the team experience?
·On a scale of 1–10, how satisfied are you with your current role and team environment?

Build your strategy plan

Based on your lowest-rated areas, build 2–3 targeted strategies. Keep each one specific, assigned, and measurable — vague plans don't get done.
