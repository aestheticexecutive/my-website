Tell us about your practice

A little context helps the AI generate values that actually sound like you — not like a corporate handbook.

Reflect on your best moments

Think about when your practice was operating at its absolute best — the days, projects, or seasons where everything clicked. These answers reveal what you actually value.

Be specific — a particular launch, a record month, a patient outcome that made everyone proud.
Think about environment, leadership, communication, the type of work involved.
e.g. "She always follows up with patients without being asked." or "He owns his mistakes immediately and fixes them."

Identify your anti-values

What you won't tolerate reveals what you truly stand for. These answers are just as important as the positive ones — they give your values real teeth.

e.g. "When people blame patients instead of owning the experience." or "Cutting corners on cleanliness."
e.g. "Someone who treats patients like transactions." or "Someone who needs to be the smartest person in the room."

Ready to generate your core values

The AI will analyze everything you've shared and craft 4–6 authentic core values — each with a memorable name, a real definition, and behaviors that show what it looks like in action.
