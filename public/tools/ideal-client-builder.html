2.2 Brand Voice

How does your brand sound when it speaks — on social media, in emails, during consultations? Examples: Polished but warm. Expert but relatable. Bold and confident. Calm and clinical.

2.3 Brand Promise

What promise are you making to every patient who walks through your doors? Examples: "You'll feel confident in your skin." / "We bring the latest science to beauty." / "Beauty meets professionalism."

2.4 Brand Experience

What should every patient feel or say after visiting you? Examples: "I felt taken care of." / "They really listened." / "That was seamless and luxurious."
