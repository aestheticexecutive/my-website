Please answer each question based on your full experience, from your initial interaction with the med spa to the completion of your visit. Be as detailed and honest as possible. Your responses will help evaluate and improve the patient journey.

1. Online Experience
1.1 Website Usability
  • Was the website easy to find through Google or another search engine?
  • Was the website mobile-friendly and easy to navigate?
  • Were you able to easily find information about services, pricing, and providers?
  • Did the website look clean, modern, and professional?
  • Was it clear how to book an appointment?
1.2 Online Booking or Inquiry Process
  • Did you book online or submit a contact form? Describe the experience.
  • How long did it take for someone to respond after submitting an inquiry (if applicable)?
  • Was the online booking process smooth and intuitive?
2. Phone Call Experience
2.1 Initial Call
  • How quickly was your call answered?
  • Was the staff member friendly, professional, and knowledgeable?
  • Did they credential the practice / provider / procedure?
  • Did they clearly explain services, pricing, and booking?
  • Did they sound confident and well-trained?
  • Were you placed on hold? If so, for how long?
  • Did any part of the call set the experience apart from a typical scheduling call?
  • Did they ask about your goals or concerns?
  • Did they help you choose the right provider or service?
  • Did they confirm the appointment details clearly?
3. Pre-Appointment Communication
  • Did you receive confirmation (text / email / call)?
  • Did you receive paperwork or instructions beforehand?
  • Were the forms easy to complete and understand?
  • Was any reminder sent before your appointment?
4. Arrival & Front Desk Experience
4.1 Check-In
  • How were you greeted upon arrival?
  • Was the check-in process smooth and organized?
  • Did the front desk staff seem informed about your appointment?
  • Were you offered anything (e.g., water, consultation form, seating)?
4.2 Wait Time
  • How long did you wait before being taken back?
  • Did the waiting area feel clean, calm, and comfortable?
5. Facility & Atmosphere
  • Was the space clean and well-maintained?
  • Did the facility feel modern and upscale?
  • Were treatment rooms private and comfortable?
  • Was there background music, lighting, scent, or ambiance that stood out?
  • Were any amenities offered (blankets, beverages, skincare samples, etc.)?
6. Consultation Experience
6.1 Staff Interaction
  • Who did you consult with? (Role, not name)
  • Did they introduce themselves and their credentials?
  • Did they ask thorough questions about your goals and concerns?
  • Did they review your medical history or contraindications?
  • Did you feel listened to and understood?
6.2 Education & Recommendations
  • Did they clearly explain the services being recommended?
  • Were risks, downtime, and expectations discussed?
  • Did they use visuals or tools to help explain procedures?
  • Were you given pricing and options clearly?
6.3 Sales Approach
  • Did it feel pushy, overly salesy, or just right?
  • Were packages or memberships mentioned? If so, how?
7. Checkout & Follow-Up
7.1 Checkout Process
  • Was the checkout process clear and professional?
  • Were pricing and charges explained?
  • Were any products or additional services offered?
7.2 Rebooking & Follow-Up
  • Were you encouraged to rebook or continue care?
  • Did anyone follow up with you after your visit?
  • Did you receive any post-visit communication (email, text, thank-you)?
8. Overall Impressions & Scoring
8.1 Overall Experience
  • What stood out as excellent?
  • What stood out as needing improvement?
